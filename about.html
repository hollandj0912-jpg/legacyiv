About Legacy IV

Built by a Builder.

Legacy IV wasn't born in a boardroom. It was born from calloused hands, long days, and a decision to build something that lasts.

The Story

From Rooftops
to Digital Infrastructure.

I've spent my entire adult life in construction — blue collar, on job sites, trading time for money. Roofing. The kind of work where you feel every hour in your body and your paycheck never seems to match the effort.

But somewhere along the way, I realized something that changed everything: the businesses I was working for had broken systems. Leads falling through the cracks. No follow-up. Websites that looked like they were built in 2009. Zero online presence. And they were bleeding money because of it.

So I started fixing it. First for myself. Then for others. I taught myself CRM systems, automation, web design, digital optimization — not from a classroom, but from doing the work. Building it. Breaking it. Rebuilding it until it was right.

That's how Legacy IV was born. Not from theory — from the trenches.

IV

Four Kids. One Legacy.

The "IV" isn't a Roman numeral for show. It represents my four kids — they're the reason behind every late night and every system I build. Legacy IV exists because I believe the work you put in today should create something that outlasts you. Something your family can be proud of.

That's not a tagline. That's the standard I hold every project to — yours included.

The Work Ethic

Blue Collar Mentality.

Construction taught me that nothing worth building comes easy. You show up, you do the work, and you don't cut corners — even when nobody's watching. That same discipline goes into every website, every CRM setup, and every system I build.

Your business gets the same intensity I'd put into my own. That's the only way I know how to operate.
